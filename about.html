About Us

Welcome to the Department of Village Services for Angaluru, Andhra Pradesh. Our mission is to empower the community by providing essential services, improving infrastructure, and fostering development in the village.

We are committed to working with the local community to ensure sustainable growth, equitable access to resources, and a better quality of life for everyone in Angaluru.

Our Vision

To transform Angaluru into a model village by fostering innovation, inclusivity, and development in all sectors.

Our Mission

  • Ensure access to clean water, sanitation, and healthcare.
  • Promote education and skill development programs.
  • Enhance agricultural productivity and sustainability.
  • Foster local entrepreneurship and employment opportunities.
